About This Application

This Environmental Justice AI integrates all major US environmental justice datasets with free AI APIs to provide accessible analysis of environmental burdens affecting vulnerable communities.

Integrated Datasets

  • EPA EJScreen - Environmental and socioeconomic indicators at census block group level
  • CDC Environmental Justice Index (EJI) - 36 environmental, social, and health indicators across 10 domains
  • Climate & Economic Justice Screening Tool (CEJST) - Disadvantaged communities identification using multiple criteria
  • FEMA National Risk Index - Climate and natural hazard risk assessment
  • DOE Energy Justice Mapping Tool - Energy burden and infrastructure analysis
  • DOT Equitable Transportation Community Explorer - Transportation equity indicators
  • CDC Social Vulnerability Index (SVI) - Community vulnerability assessment
  • NOAA Climate Mapping for Resilience & Adaptation (CMRA) - Climate resilience data

Free AI APIs Used

  • Google Gemini - 15 RPM free tier, multimodal capabilities
  • Groq - Up to 14,400 requests/day, no credit card required
  • Mistral AI - 1 billion tokens/month free tier
